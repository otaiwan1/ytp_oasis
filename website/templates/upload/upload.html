Upload Guide
File Format

Only .stl (STereoLithography) files are accepted. These are standard 3D dental scan exports.

Naming Convention

Files are stored as patientUID_serialNumber.stl. Serial numbers are auto-assigned.

Multiple Scans

Upload multiple scans for the same patient at once. They'll be indexed sequentially.

Auto-Indexing

After upload, the embeddings cache is refreshed so new scans are searchable immediately.
